Edit-in: Show, Don't Tell
28 Jun, 2021 7 PM - 9 PM Virtual
Edit-ins are for writers who are interested in collaborative editing that is focused on a specific technical point of interest. This edit-in will focus on ways of achieving the oft-given advice: "Show, Don't Tell." For ideas on this topic, check out K.M. Weiland's post.
